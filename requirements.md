# ContentFlow AI — Requirements Document

## 1. Project Overview

ContentFlow AI is an AI-driven Intelligent Content Workflow Automation platform that transforms a single content input into multiple platform-optimized outputs automatically.

The system automates the entire content lifecycle:

Idea → Creation → Transformation → Distribution → Optimization

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Content creators and businesses face the following challenges:

- Content creation is fragmented across platforms (Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, Blogs).
- Manual content adaptation for different formats is time-consuming.
- No predictive insights before posting.
- Engagement tracking and optimization require manual effort.

---

## 3. Objectives

- Automate content transformation across multiple platforms.
- Provide AI-based engagement prediction before publishing.
- Enable scheduling and performance tracking in one dashboard.
- Reduce manual marketing effort by 70%.

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1 User Management
- User registration and login
- Role-based access (Creator, Business, Admin)
- Profile management
- Subscription management (Free, Pro, Enterprise)

### 4.2 Content Upload
- Upload video files
- Upload audio files
- Upload text content
- Support Indian regional languages

### 4.3 AI Content Processing
- Speech-to-text conversion
- AI summarization
- Content transformation for:
  - Instagram
  - LinkedIn
  - YouTube
  - Blog
- Caption generation
- Hashtag generation
- Platform-specific formatting

### 4.4 Engagement Prediction
- AI engagement score prediction
- Content optimization suggestions
- Best posting time recommendation

### 4.5 Scheduling & Publishing
- Calendar-based scheduling
- Multi-platform auto-publishing
- Post preview before publishing

### 4.6 Analytics & Feedback
- Engagement metrics tracking
- Performance dashboard
- AI learning from historical performance
- A/B testing support (Future)

---
